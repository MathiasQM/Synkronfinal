Døssing

Process:

Døssing is a danish jazz trio from Aarhus consisting of the siblings Mathilde, Jon og Aske Døssing Bendixen. The trio's sound is electro-jazzed and synth-driven instrumental music that bridges different jazz traditions, involving dubbed grooves and some rather catchy melodies that stick. The soft piano sound, the dusty percussion loops and the laid-back trombone create a minimalist lounge aesthetic.

As their album ‘All Inclusive’ is shaped around arriving at a hotel we set out to explore this vivid universe which the trio had built up over the years and visualize it in sync with their music.

Products:

Welcome to Hotel All Inclusive. We would like to invite you on a journey between people, mystery and magic.

In our efforts to describe what went on in Hotel All Inclusive, its inhabitants, its sensory landscapes and sound we had a series of talks with the trio, exchanging ideas and developing a number of concepts to set the artist's direction. The result was a series of creative productions manifesting the ideas, mood and aesthetic of the trio.

These productions included: concept development and copywriting in relation to a short film anthology, Promotional materials in a series of short scenes, press material in the form of a series of photoshoots as well as a live session and a number of coverarts for the singles ‘all inclusive’ & ‘chai wallah Shub’.
